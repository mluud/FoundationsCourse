The Pollinator Highway User Research

Intangible Cultural Heritage

The Center of Anthropology, 2023

Aiming to find out the users of the List of Intangible Cultural Heritage website and how they use the website in order to find shortcomings of the current website for future improvements on the user experience.

The Center of Anthropology, 2023

Aiming to understand the impact and level of implementation of the Estonian Diversity Charter (founded by the Estonian Human Rights Centre), as well as the needs of employers in the process of implementing the initiative.
